About Me

My hometown’s endless waves and golden beaches have shaped my curiosity and creativity, always inspiring me to explore the unknown. "The ocean is more than just water; it's a symbol of infinite possibilities."

Whether it’s developing web applications, training AI models, or analyzing data, I enjoy the challenge of solving complex problems. 🔹 Web Development (React, Node.js, Bootstrap) 🔹 Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (Python, TensorFlow) 🔹 UI/UX Design (Figma, Photoshop) "I believe that technology should not only be functional but also intuitive and beautiful."
