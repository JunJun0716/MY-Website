Celebrating Spring Festival

Spring Festival in New York

The Spring Festival (Chinese New Year) is the most important traditional festival in China, symbolizing reunion, prosperity, and the start of a new year. Families usually gather, enjoy big feasts, and watch fireworks to welcome the new year. This year, as an international student, I celebrated Spring Festival in New York with my friends. We rented an apartment together and made it feel like home. We cooked traditional Chinese dishes, played card games, and shared lots of laughter.
